ABOUT UNIVERSITY COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

University College of Engineering (UCOE) is an engineering college located at Punjabi University campus in Patiala district of Punjab. The college was established in the year 2003 by the team of experts including the senior faculty members of the university and the vice-chancellor, with the objective of providing quality technical education in the state of Punjab. The college is a part of Punjabi university, established in the year 1962 and has been accredited with 5 star status by NAAC. The college organizes various activities throughout the year. Some of them include: social and cultural events, tech fest, sports competition etc. Association of computing Machinery at Punjabi University, patiala, acm pup, is a student-run organization dedicated to advancing computing technology and educating students at the field of computing machinery. We are a supportive, diverse community of students coming together to bridge the gap between the academic and professional world. Acm-pup, established in the year 2019. The acm student chapter primarily focus on creating interest, developing the logical thinking ability and spreading awareness about the techological and computing information among the people. Started as small unit, the ACM-PUP team believes in working together as a family and always standing as a constant support for each other. We work as hard as possible and put all our hearts and sweat to make sure that it functions properly and stands to people's expectation. Our goal is to help our students developing their technical and professional skills which help them in school and in industry. We aim to accomplish our goals by providing technical workshops, professional events, and providing opportunities for our members to excel.

ABOUT ACM

One of Acm's most important role is maintaining a network of communication between students, the University and the corporate world. We want to improve our members professionally, technically and personally. We want to help our members grow throughout their college career, whether they enter as a first-year university student or as a transfer student. We develop the professional resumes, build all-star linkedlns, and offers workshops on Applying effectively at companies from big 4 to startups. We introduce technical pathways ranging from web/app development to security to cloud computing. We supplement the curriculum with workshops to tailored to our members and their goals. But,more importantly, we want our members to make positive attributes through software. The success of Acm-pup can only be measured by the success of our members.
